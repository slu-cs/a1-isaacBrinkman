Voter Requirements

Qualifications:

1. You must be a U.S. citizen

2. You must be 18 years old by December 31st of the year in which you file your registration form (note - you must be 18 years old by the date of the general, primary, or other election in which you want to vote)

3. You must be a resident of St. Lawrence County for at least 30 days prior to the election

4. You must not be in prison or on parole for a felony conviction (unless parolee pardoned or restored rights of citizenship)

5. You must not be adjudged mentally incompetent by a court

6. You must not be claiming the right to vote elsewhere

Early voting will begin at the canton board of elections office in the two weeks before upcoming special, primary, or general elections.
